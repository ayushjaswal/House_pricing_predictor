About This Project

This project utilizes a Linear Regression model for predicting house prices. The model is based on Linear Regression and Lasso techniques with cross validation. We are continuously improving its accuracy to provide better predictions.

Parameters Used

To predict the price of a house, our model takes into account the following parameters:

  • Area: The total area of the house.
  • Bedrooms: The number of bedrooms in the house.
  • Bathrooms: The number of bathrooms in the house.
  • Stories: The number of stories the house has.
  • Main Road: Whether the house is located on a main road.
  • Guestroom: Whether the house has a guestroom.
  • Basement: Whether the house has a basement.
  • Hot Water Heating: Whether the house has hot water heating.
  • Air Conditioning: Whether the house has air conditioning.
  • Parking: The number of parking spaces available.
  • Pref Area: Whether the house is in a preferred area.
  • Furnishing Status: The furnishing status of the house.
